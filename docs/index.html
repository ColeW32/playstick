Stick.ai
How it works
For the FlightScope Mevo+

Tour-level practice.
Your launch monitor.

Stick.ai turns your Mevo+ into a TrackMan-grade practice simulator — real ball flight from every shot, strokes-gained scoring, and a practice handicap that actually tracks your game.

Download for Windows
Free · Windows 10 & 11 · one-click installer
Why Stick.ai

Real ball flight

Every shot flies on your measured carry, spin, apex and offline — then bounces, checks, or zips back on the green just like the real thing.

Strokes-gained scoring

Every shot is graded against PGA-Tour baselines, so you know instantly whether that approach helped or hurt.

Practice handicap

A rolling handicap and session stats — GIR, proximity, trends — so practice finally shows whether you're improving.

Up and running in a minute

Install

Download and double-click Stick-Setup.exe. Stick.ai lands on your desktop — no setup, no admin.

Connect your Mevo+

Put your PC on the Mevo's Wi-Fi and power it on. Open Stick.ai — it finds the monitor automatically.

Hit shots

Every shot plays out in 3D with full data and a score. No launch monitor handy? Open Stick.ai Demo to try it.

Works with: FlightScope Mevo+  ·  Needs: Windows 10/11 PC on the same Wi-Fi as the monitor
